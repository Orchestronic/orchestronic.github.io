Relax and Reflect

Mia ( Unknown )

Take full thy breath, and gentle thy mind.

The real is imagination, and magic's all around.

Tell the rest of your worries they can wait in line.

Let those love-lorn faces fall behind.

Of wind and leaf-drop and conversation, how sweet the sound.

Take full thy breath, and gentle thy mind.

Now, dear, warm the bread and pour the wine.

Vineyard, farmer, and plantation- secret stories to be found.

Tell the rest of your worries they can wait in line.
